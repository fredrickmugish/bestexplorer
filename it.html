10-Day Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in New York City

Explore Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty.

Day 2: Broadway and Museums

Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art and enjoy a Broadway show.

Day 3: Trip to Washington D.C.

Tour the White House, Lincoln Memorial, and Smithsonian Museums.

Day 4: Niagara Falls

Experience the breathtaking views of Niagara Falls.

Day 5: Chicago City Tour

Visit Millennium Park, Willis Tower, and Navy Pier.

Day 6: Grand Canyon

Explore the South Rim and enjoy a helicopter tour.

Day 7: Las Vegas

Experience the Las Vegas Strip and enjoy a live show.

Day 8: San Francisco

Visit the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, and Fisherman's Wharf.

Day 9: Los Angeles

Explore Hollywood, Beverly Hills, and Santa Monica.

Day 10: Departure

Transfer to the airport for your flight back home.
