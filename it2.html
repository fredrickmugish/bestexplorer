10-Day Travel Itinerary

Discover the best destinations, activities, and experiences with BestExplorerTours. Here’s a detailed day-by-day plan for your adventure.

Day 1: Arrival in New York City

Welcome to the Big Apple! Start your journey with a visit to iconic landmarks like Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty. Enjoy a delicious dinner at a rooftop restaurant with stunning city views.

  • Visit Times Square
  • Explore Central Park
  • Statue of Liberty Tour

Day 2: Broadway and Museums

Immerse yourself in culture with a visit to the Metropolitan Museum of Art. In the evening, enjoy a world-class Broadway show. End the day with a stroll through the vibrant streets of Manhattan.

  • Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • Broadway Show
  • Manhattan Night Walk

Day 3: Trip to Washington D.C.

Travel to the nation’s capital and explore the White House, Lincoln Memorial, and Smithsonian Museums. Learn about American history and culture in this iconic city.

  • White House Tour
  • Lincoln Memorial Visit
  • Smithsonian Museums
